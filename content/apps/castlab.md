CastLab lets you stream photos, videos, and music from your phone to any DLNA/UPnP or FCast compatible device on your local network. Browse your gallery, build playlists, extract videos from the web, and cast everything to your TV or speakers.

## Supported devices

CastLab works with any device that supports DLNA/UPnP or FCast, including smart TVs, media players, Chromecast alternatives, and network speakers. Devices are discovered automatically on your local network.

## Key features

- **Gallery casting**: browse your photos, videos, and music by album and cast them to any compatible device
- **Playlists**: create custom playlists with autoplay and background playback
- **Video extraction**: extract and cast videos from web pages, PeerTube, and YouTube
- **IPTV support**: import M3U/M3U8 playlists for online streams
- **Screen sharing**: mirror your Android screen to a DLNA device
- **Privacy-friendly**: YouTube videos are routed through Invidious instances
- **Playback controls**: play, pause, seek, and volume from your phone
- **Themes**: Light, Dark, Auto, and 8 custom color presets with Material Design 3
- **Translations**: community-translated via Weblate in 18+ languages
