FediPlan App

Android / iOS
Mobile React Native GPL v3
code Source code android F-Droid

FediPlan App is the mobile companion to FediPlan. Schedule posts on Mastodon and Pleroma directly from your phone. Pick a date and time, compose your message, and FediPlan App takes care of publishing it for you.

Key features

  • Multi-account: schedule posts across all your Fediverse accounts
  • Date and time picker: calendar and clock interface to choose when to publish
  • Post editor: full compose screen with character counter respecting instance limits
  • Media support: attach images with cropping, alt text descriptions, and sensitive media toggle
  • Polls: create polls with multiple options
  • Content warnings: add spoiler text to your scheduled posts
  • Emoji picker: standard and custom instance emoji
  • Visibility control: public, unlisted, private, or direct
  • Language selection: tag your posts with the right language
  • Manage scheduled posts: view, edit, and delete upcoming posts
  • Pleroma support: compatible with Pleroma-specific features like content formats
  • Themes: Light, Dark, and Auto
  • Translations: available in English, French, German, Russian, Turkish, and Chinese
